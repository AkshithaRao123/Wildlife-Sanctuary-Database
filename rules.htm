Rules and regulations
Dear Visitors, Greetings !
Kindly note the following information:
Tickets can be purchased through Online mode for Zoo, Safari, Butterfly park and Parking.
A family is considered as a group.
Types of tickets available
    a) Jeep Safari Combo ( Safari + Zoo + Butterfly Park )
    b) AC Bus Safari Combo ( Safari + Zoo + Butterfly Park )
    c) Non AC Bus Safari Combo ( Safari + Zoo )
    d) Zoo only
    e) Butterfly Park only
    f) Parking, Luggage, fine and others.

Parking tickets are mandatory for own and rented vehicles. Children below 5 years have free entry (Not applicable to school groups). Senior Citizen, Specially abled persons have concession.
Limited number of online tickets are available for Zoo , Butterfly Park , Safari as per the COVID -19 revised carrying capacity estimation done at the park in order to maintain social distancing norms.
Your temperature will be checked using digital thermometer at the gates prior to entry. In case of high body temperature or any other symptoms related to COVID-19 in any one of the group members, all members of a group will be denied entry to the park. In such cases payment will not be refunded. Updated MHA, GoI and GoK guidelines to be followed.
Visitors must report 15 minutes prior to booked time. You are requested to strictly follow the timings printed on your tickets to be allowed entry. Waiting time for Safari is about 30 minutes. However due to heavy crowd during holidays, it is likely to get extended. Visitors are required to cooperate with the management.
Facilities: Toilets, resting places, hand sanitizers, cloak room, wheelchairs etc.,
We Encourage Visitors
  • To carry face masks, hand sanitizer, gloves, water bottle, snacks & lunch, umbrella and wear cap, comfortable shoes as you are required to walk about 2.7 Km.
  • To bring home made food and have it at the places designated for the purpose . ( Hill view Restaurant & Hotel at BMTC building ).
    • Restaurant are operational as per government guidelines.
    Disinfection Protocol
  • Hand Sanitizers are placed at entry gate and various places around the park.
  • All safari vehicles and battery operated vehicles will be sanitized after every trip.
  • Parking premises, standoff barriers and other common facilities will be disinfected regularly.

    • Penal Provision
  • Heavy Fine will be imposed for spitting or teasing / feeding animals and any other violation.
  • Ministry of Home Affairs, GoI and Government of Karnataka COVID 19 guidelines to be strictly followed
  • All visitors shall strictly follow the directions issued by the BBP. Do’s and Don’ts displayed at the park to be strictly followed.
